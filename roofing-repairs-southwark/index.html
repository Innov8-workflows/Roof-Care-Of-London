A small roof problem never stays small. Roofing Repairs Southwark — common questions

How quickly can you repair a roof in Southwark?

For urgent leaks we aim to make the roof safe within days, with the permanent repair scheduled straight after. Most standard repairs in Southwark are completed within one to two weeks of your free survey.

Will the repair match my existing roof?

Yes — we source reclaimed slates and matching tiles wherever possible so the repair blends in, which matters on the period streets we work on every day.

Is it worth repairing, or do I need a new roof?

We’ll tell you straight. If a repair will genuinely last, that’s what we quote. If the roof is at the end of its life we’ll explain why, show you photos, and price a re-roof alongside so you can compare.
