Led from the top, on the tools

Roof Care of London Ltd is led by founder James Smith, who has spent 20 years working on London’s roofs — from Victorian terraces in Battersea and Clapham to listed properties in Kensington, Chelsea and Westminster. James still leads projects personally, which is why the standard doesn’t slip between the quote and the finished roof.

The team behind him is 10 strong: experienced roofers, leadworkers and bricklayers who’ve worked together for years. We don’t hand your job to unknown subcontractors, and we don’t disappear when the deposit clears. Every project carries £5 million public liability insurance and a workmanship guarantee.

We’re based at Warwick Chambers, Pater Street, London W8 6EW — in the heart of the area we serve. Our reviews on Checkatrade and MyBuilder say more than we ever could: honest pricing, tidy sites, and roofs that stay fixed.
